Making Homemade Pizza With a Gas Pizza Oven

I think this name gives an accurate description of what I'm trying to accomplish here. This site will be focused on my methods and recipes for making homemade pizz in a gas pizza oven.

Purpose: To help people who are new to pizza making

I spent a lot of time researching and practicing the basics. I feel a site like this can save people time and frustration when starting their own journy. I will give my recipes with a calculator to allow for any batch size, and will show some of my methods of cooking.

Senarios

  • What temperature should I cook the pizzas at?
  • What's a good recipe for crust?
  • What's a good recipe for sauce?
  • What equipment should I have?
  • What's some advice that will help me succeed?

Color Schema

The color schema that I've picked consists of...

  • #bc433b for the headings
  • #5c5b59 for the text
  • #eae7dc for the page background
  • #d8c3a5 for backgrounds of cards, lists, or paragraphs that need emphasis

I've used these colors here on this page where I will on the final site.

Typography

I'm planning on using "Barrio" for the headings. I think it gives it a fun casual feel.

And I'll be using "Zain" for the regular text. Paragraphs, list items, image captions, and the like. It's still very readable, but not as formal as many other fonts.
