Directions

Sift the flours together into a bowl of a stand mixer.

Sprinkle in the salt and yeast.

Whisk everything together until homologous.

Add the water to the bowl. Start mixing it by hand using the dough hook. Once all of the water has been absorbed by the flour attach the bowl and hook to the mixer.

Kneed the dough for 7 minuets.

Remove the dough hook and transfer the dough to a lightly greased plastic tub with plenty of room for growth.

Optional: Let the dough rise for an hour or two at room temperature.

Place the tub in the fridge and let it rise for at least 12 hours. It can stay in the fridge for up to a week.
